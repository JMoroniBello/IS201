The outdoors

I love the outdoors. Some of my favorite things to do are rock climbing, fishing and camping. I spent countless weekends out in the middle of nowehere camping with my dad growing up and I have just really fallen in love with the outdoors.

The beach

My favorite place on earth is the beach. If I could live anywhere on earth, I would live on the beach in florida. To me, a vacation isnt a vacation without a little sand and some serious sun.

Paraguay

I had the opportunity to serve a 2 year mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints in Paraguay. I absolutely loved my mission and the people I met there. I learned so much thorugh that experience, including spanish and time management skills. I think the most defining experience of my life was the time I spent serving my mission.
